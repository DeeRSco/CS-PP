Support Services

Finding the right support can make all the difference when dealing with intimate partner violence. 8th Season is here to connect you with services that offer the assistance you need to navigate this challenging time.

Steps for Effective Safety Planning:

  1. Shelters and Housing: Find safe temporary housing for you and your children.
    2. Establish a Safe Space: Identify safe areas in your home where there are no weapons and where you can quickly escape.
  2. Legal Assistance: Get help with restraining orders, custody issues, and other legal matters.
  3. Counseling and Therapy: Receive emotional support and mental health services from qualified professionals.
  4. Case Management: Get assistance from professionals to provide support, resources, and guidance in decision-making.
  5. Support Groups: Connect with others who have experienced similar situations for mutual support and encouragement

Online Resources:

  • National Domestic Violence Hotline
  • Rape, Abuse & Incest National Network (RAINN)
  • National Coalition Against Domestic Violence (NCADV)
  • Domestic Shelters
  • Legal Aid Society

Disclaimer:

At 8th Season, we believe that in an ideal world, police and the prison system would not exist. However, one of the main indicators of lethal intimate partner violence is a repetitive pattern of violent acts. The information provided here is not meant to be punitive or judgmental. It is intended solely as a resource to help individuals make informed decisions and seek the help they need.
