About 8th Season

8th Season was created with a single purpose: to empower individuals facing the threat of intimate partner violence by providing critical information and resources. We understand the complexities and dangers involved in these situations, and our goal is to offer a comprehensive hub where you can find the support you need.

Our Mission:

To reduce the risk of future violence by offering detailed insights and access to public information on violent crime arrests and restraining orders.

Why We Exist:

The risk of intimate partner violence is significantly higher for those with a history of witnessing or experiencing abuse. Alcohol problems in aggressors can increase the incidence of violence by up to four times. A staggering 55% of all homicides are related to intimate partner violence. Access to weapons amplifies the lethality of such violence by six times. 8th Season is here to shine a light on these stark realities and provide a pathway to safety and understanding.

Disclaimer:

At 8th Season, we believe that in an ideal world, police and the prison system would not exist. However, one of the main indicators of lethal intimate partner violence is a repetitive pattern of violent acts. The information provided here is not meant to be punitive or judgmental. It is intended solely as a resource to help individuals make informed decisions and seek the help they need.
